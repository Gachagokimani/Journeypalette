    Wildlife plays a vital role in providing ecosystem services such as pollination, seed dispersal, and nutrient cycling.Animals often feature in folklore, stories, and as national symbols, reflecting their importance to our African culture. As human populations expand, conflicts with wildlife can and do occur, leading to challenges in coexistence but in our case we have dedicated wildlife service that see that the boundary between human and animals is respected which also plays a huge role in the efforts to quell poaching which is detrimental to the existence of wildlife as a whole.Wildlife Tourism- This can be a significant source of revenue for conservation but must be managed to ensure it doesn’t harm the animals or their habitats but help at conserving efforts that aim to see that this precious resource are harnessed in the best way both benefiting the environment and biodiversity that is accorded to us by mother nature.
